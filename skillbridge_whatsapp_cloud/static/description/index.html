FAQ

What do I need from Meta?

You need a WhatsApp Business Cloud app, permanent token, phone_number_id, verify token, and app secret.

Does it honor opt-in?

Yes. Non-opted partners are blocked and logged as failed.

Can I track delivery?

Yes. All statuses (sent, delivered, read, failed) are logged and accessible through pivot views.

Is it multi-company?

Yes. Each company uses its own WhatsApp credentials.
