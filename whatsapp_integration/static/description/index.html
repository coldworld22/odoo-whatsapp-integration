How it works

1) Connect Meta
Add token, phone number ID, verify token, and app secret in Settings. Create a default WhatsApp Account per company.
2) Templates & audience
Sync Meta-approved templates, mark them APPROVED, and define partner tags/domains for campaigns.
3) Send or automate
Use “Send WhatsApp” on Sales Orders or run campaigns with queue generation, drip steps, and throttled batches.

FAQ

What do I need from Meta?

A WhatsApp Business Cloud app, permanent token with whatsapp_business_messaging, phone number ID, webhook verify token, and app secret.

Does it honor opt-in?

Yes. Partners have an opt-in flag; invalid numbers or no opt-in are skipped and logged as failed.

Can I track deliveries?

Outbound logs capture sent/delivered/read/failed. Pivot view summarizes KPIs per campaign/partner.

Is it multi-company?

Yes. Use per-company WhatsApp Accounts for tokens and phone number IDs; no hard-coded credentials.
