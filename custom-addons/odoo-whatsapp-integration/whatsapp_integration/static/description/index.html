WhatsApp Business Cloud

WhatsApp for Sales, Invoices, Campaigns, and Inbound Service

Send and automate WhatsApp messages directly from Odoo with templates, interactive buttons/lists, media, PDFs, campaigns, and delivery tracking. Built for compliance, multi-company, and sales teams that need traceability.

Sales Orders & Invoices with PDFs Templates + Variables + Compliance Hints Interactive Buttons & Lists Campaign Drips & Throttling Inbound Logs + Activities

Message Types

  • Text, templates (Meta), images, documents (PDF), interactive buttons, and lists.
  • Template variables with validation; language + category metadata for audits.
  • Attach Sales Order PDFs and posted invoices in one click.

Automation & Campaigns

  • Auto-send on Sales Order confirmation or Invoice post (optional).
  • Campaign queues with drip steps, delay hours, send windows, and throttling.
  • Retries with backoff; webhook-driven failure handling to stop bad drips.

Compliance & Data Quality

  • E.164 validation and opt-in flags on partners.
  • Per-company WhatsApp accounts and tokens; no hard-coded credentials.
  • Webhook signature validation (X-Hub-Signature-256) with verify token and app secret.

Observability

  • Message logs with status (sent/delivered/read/failed) and pivots.
  • Inbound messages stored with payload trace; chatter posts on orders/partners.
  • Keyword actions (PAY/HELP/CALL) create activities for owners.
How it works
Configure token, phone number ID, verify token, and app secret. Add templates/campaigns. From a Sales Order click Send WhatsApp, pick text/template/interactive/media, and attach PDFs or invoices automatically.
Requirements
Odoo 15/16/17; WhatsApp Business Cloud API with permanent token + phone number ID; customer mobiles in E.164 and opt-in captured.
Good to know
Cron-based campaign batches every 10 minutes; per-company account fallback; activity alerts on delivery failures; default media URL supported for quick sends.
